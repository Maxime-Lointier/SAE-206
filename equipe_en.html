Team

Who are we ?

We are a team of 6 computer science students. As part of our training, we were led to discover a cultural event and then create a website as well as a poster for a 2025 edition of it. Ainsi notre choix s’est porté sur ...

Karanba - project chief

He assigns tasks, supervises the progress of the project, writes the worksheet route, formats the logbook and returns the deliverables to the teacher.

Manel - Communication manager

She takes care of making contact with those responsible for the event by writing an email, calling or simply reserving one or more place(s). She does the interview by asking the questions. Takes the photos and film, paying attention to the rights image.

Jorawar - Designer poster

The one who will design the poster for the 2025 event and also make the program of this poster 2025.

Maxime et Mathew - Site designer

They bring together texts, images, videos and other content of the exhibition by integrating them into the site. They will design a visual style using colors, fonts and images that reflect the atmosphere of the exhibition. Collect feedback and suggestions from the entire team for improvements. They will use CSS for this work.

Yossra - Site developer

In this project I am responsible for carrying out the development part of the project. This will format the site.Creation models by visualizing the different pages and interactions of the website. She will design the architecture of the future website by organizing the exhibition information and determining the different categories of the site. (in 3 pages) It will mainly use HTML and JavaScript languages.
