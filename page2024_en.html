Al Thani collection

Exhibition summary


the Al Thani collection includes an exceptional collection of works of art spanning a long period from antiquity to the present day. Encyclopedic in its approach and representative of a rich range of cultures and civilizations, the Collection celebrates the creativity and universal power of art through the ages.

The Collection is presented by the Al Thani Collection Foundation, a non-profit organization whose main mission is to encourage and promote art and culture.

Since November 2021, the centerpieces of the Al Thani collection are exhibited to the public in a dedicated museum space of the Hôtel de la Marine, in Paris. The galleries also host biennial thematic exhibitions and loans from partner museums around the world, as well as educational events and seminars.
